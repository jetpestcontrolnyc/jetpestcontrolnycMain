About Us

New York City is well known for skyscrapers, museums, and an incredibly fast-paced lifestyle. Unfortunately, people are not the only ones on the move in the Big Apple. Bed Bug infestations seem to happen overnight. If you find one, rush to the phone and call Jet Pest Control. As the #1 Bed Bug removal service in New York City and the five boroughs, we work hard to take care of your issue as fast as possible.

Even though the social stigmas associated with the nasty creature are inaccurate, we are still aware of them and make sure our visit to your home or office is as discreet as possible. Our eco-friendly service is not limited to bed bugs; ants, roaches, rodents, and more are also on our hitlist. We are licensed and insured and treat your home in the most responsible way possible.
